Health Insurance

  • High Concept: Avoid traffic as long as you can.
  • Player Experience Goals: Survival, dodging.
  • Genre: Aracde.
  • Game play: Navigate through the traffic and avoid getting into an accident.
  • Prototype goal: Collision detection, traffic aversion.
  • Features: Timed survival gameplay.
  • Setting/Premise: The freeway.
  • Story: You just won the lottery and can finally afford to pay for your wife's surgery. You need to rush to the hospital as fast as you can before it's too late.
  • Target Audience: Casuals.
  • Play time: Varies
  • Strategies: Anticipating the paths of the cars to make short term or long term judgements in movement. You could stay on the right side, which has slower moving, but more, cars. Or you could stayon the left side, which has less fast-moving cars. Staying in the right side provides more safety, but could end up trapping you in.
  • Prototype Assets: I drew the cars and background myself. There's an ambient traffic sound that I opted for instead of music.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Street Race.
